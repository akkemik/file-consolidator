Cookie Policy

Effective Date: 2026-03-15

This Cookie Policy explains how Murat Akkemik uses cookies and similar technologies on File Consolidator.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, remember preferences, improve performance, and support analytics and advertising.

2. Local File Processing Notice

The Website's file consolidation and processing features operate entirely locally within the user's browser on the user's own device. Files and file contents are not uploaded to our servers, not processed server-side, and not stored by us.

Cookies and similar technologies used on this Website relate to website functionality, consent preferences, analytics, and advertising where applicable — not to the storage of user file contents processed locally in the browser.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

a) Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are required for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled in our systems. They may be used for security, basic functionality, or consent management.

b) Functional Cookies

These cookies help remember user preferences and improve usability.

c) Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors use the website, such as which pages are visited and how users interact with the site.

d) Advertising Cookies

These cookies may be used by us or our advertising partners, such as Google AdSense, to display ads, measure ad performance, limit repeated ads, and where allowed personalize advertising.

4. Consent

If you are located in a jurisdiction where consent is required for non-essential cookies, we will ask for your consent before using analytics or advertising cookies.

You may accept, reject, or manage your cookie preferences through our cookie banner or consent management platform.

5. Google Advertising Cookies

We may use Google AdSense or other Google advertising products on this website. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads based on your visit to this and other websites, measure ad performance, prevent fraud and abuse, and personalize ads where you have given valid consent.

If required by law, Google advertising cookies will only be used after you have provided the necessary consent.

6. Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through our cookie consent banner or preference center, through your browser settings, and through third-party opt-out tools where available.

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

7. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be placed by third-party services integrated into the website, such as advertising, analytics, hosting, or embedded content providers. We do not control all third-party cookies directly.
