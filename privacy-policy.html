https://akkemik.github.io/file-consolidator/

1. Who We Are

Website owner: Murat Akkemik
Contact email: info@akkemik.net
Country: Sweden

2. Local Processing of Files

All file processing takes place locally in the user's browser on the user's own device. Files, documents, and data uploaded or selected by the user for consolidation or processing are not uploaded to our servers, not processed on our servers, and not stored by us.

This means that, as part of the core file processing functionality of the Website:

  • your files remain on your local device;
  • file contents are processed locally within your browser environment;
  • we do not receive, access, store, or review the contents of your files; and
  • we do not maintain server-side storage of files or processed output generated through the Website's local processing tools.

However, like most websites, we and our service providers may still process limited technical data such as IP address, browser information, consent preferences, and advertising-related data where applicable.

3. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Usage data, such as browser type, device type, pages visited, time spent on the site, referring pages, and general interaction data.
  • Technical data, such as IP address, browser language, operating system, and basic diagnostic information.
  • Advertising and consent data, including preferences related to cookies, consent choices, and ad-related identifiers where permitted.
  • Communications data, if you contact us directly by email.

We do not collect the contents of files processed locally through the Website's browser-based tools.
